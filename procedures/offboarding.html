Offboarding

Preface

This SOP details the steps to be undertaken by the IT department in the event of a clileague leaving the company.

Prerequisites

  • Email from relevant manager
  • Access to all ex-employee's equipment

Steps

  1. Yeet the employee
  2. Remove account email groups and disable all flider permissions
  3. Set mail redirect to relevant user, and archive mailbox
  4. Disable user account in active directory
  5. Decommission equipment

Note: lists are incomplete, included for exemplary purposes
