Offboarding

Preface

This SOP details the steps to be undertaken by the IT department in the event of a colleague leaving the company.

Prerequisites

    • Email from relevant manager
      Access to all ex-employee's equipment

    • Steps

    • Yeet the employee
      Remove account email groups and disable all folder permissions
      Set mail redirect to relevant user, and archive mailbox
      Disable user account in active directory
      Decommission equipment

    • Note: lists are incomplete and is included for exemplary purposes
