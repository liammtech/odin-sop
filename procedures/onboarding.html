Onboarding

Preface

This SOP details the steps to be undertaken by the IT department in the event of a new colleague joining the company.

Prerequisites

  • Email from relevant manager
  • Computer (+ relevant cables)
  • Monitor screens (+ relevant cables)
  • Monitor stand
  • Mouse
  • Keyboard
  • Unoccupied workstation

Steps

  1. Read email from relevant manager, taking into account any details relevant to the user's setup, including name and job title. They should also inform you when the new starter is due to arrive
  2. Take equipment to allocated unoccupied workstation
  3. Attach or place monitor stand onto workstation, and set to suitable position
  4. Attach monitor screens to stand, adjusting position if necessary
  5. Place PC in suitable position relative to monitors, where the user will have enough desk room to coordinate their daily tasks. Ensure that cables can reach monitors, network and power supply
  6. Attach all cabling
  7. Attach any peripherals: mouse and keyboard
  8. Ensure network and power supply are active
  9. Test PC workstation

Note: lists are incomplete, included for exemplary purposes
