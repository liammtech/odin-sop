Onboarding

Preface

This SOP details the steps to be undertaken by the IT department in the event of a new colleague joining the company.

Prerequisites

    • Email from relevant manager
      Computer (+ relevant cables)
      Monitor screens (+ relevant cables)
      Monitor stand
      Mouse
      Keyboard
      Unoccupied workstation

    • Steps

    • Read email from relevant manager, taking into account any details relevant to the user's setup, including name and job title. They should also inform you when the new starter is due to arrive
      Take equipment to allocated unoccupied workstation
      Attach or place monitor stand onto workstation, and set to suitable position
      Attach monitor screens to stand, adjusting position if necessary
      Place PC in suitable position relative to monitors, where the user will have enough desk room to coordinate their daily tasks. Ensure that cables can reach monitors, network and power supply
      Attach all cabling
      Attach any peripherals: mouse and keyboard
      Ensure network and power supply are active
      Test PC workstation
